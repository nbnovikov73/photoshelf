# PhotoShelf Manifesto

Photography is more important than the platform.

The archive is more important than the feed.

A selected photograph deserves a quiet place.

The photographer should own the work, the structure, and the presentation.

A personal website should not punish the author with CMS friction.

Publishing should feel almost invisible.

One strong image is better than a hundred forgettable posts.

PhotoShelf exists to make the personal photography website useful again.
